About us

Man’s desire to share his experience has given birth to many a temple of knowledge which functions not only as seats of learning but also as guardians of progress in all walks of life. These centers of learning are responsible to generate, disseminate and spread knowledge in the domain of science and technology, human values and socio-economic pursuits.

C. V. Raman College of Engineering is a progressive institution in Orissa, set up to promote the highest standards of technical education in the state. The institution aims at molding a new breed of technocrats with a competitive edge to match both national and international standards. The college aims at educating the students to become not only competent professionals but also excellent human beings who would contribute towards the welfare of the society and help in raising the quality of life of its people..

Departments

MAJOR DEPARTMENTS OF CV RAMAN ARE

COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

This is a period of glory for the Computer Science and Engineering profession as the rapidly changing technology creates many opportunities and challenges. Department of Computer Science and Engineering is prepared to meet the challenges and is playing a leadership role in shaping the education of the 21st Century by providing unique educational and research opportunities in the forefront of Computer Science and Engineering. The department of Computer Science and Engineering established in the year 1997 offers UG programs in Computer Science and Engineering along with PG programs in Computer Science & Engineering.

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information technology plays a crucial role in coordinating the diverse operations of various industries. The department provides solution-based training which links to the productivity gains. Its strategy is to help the students to improve their performance by building, supporting, optimizing and delivering innovative solutions. The course covers the key technology of software industry that includes client server, networking, database which meet the needs of ever growing challenges in an IT driven business environment. The cross platform technologies and highly skilled and qualified faculties facilitate the students to build their career and competencies as per the current market demands.

ELECTRONICS AND TELECOM ENGINEERING

The Department is offering B. Tech and M.Tech in “Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering”. Due to the dedicated involvement of faculty members and students, R and D and consultancy activities of the department in terms of funded projects, publications, collaborative projects and consultancies have excelled during the last decade. The department has been carrying out R&D activities in diverse areas such as Adhoc Networks, Mobile Communication, Smart Antenna, Microwave Engineering, Analog VLSI, Pattern Recognition, VLSI Implementation of DSP and Communication System and Image Analysis and Computer Vision.

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

Electrical Engineering Department was established in the Year 2002 with a sanctioned strength of 60 students and increased to 120 in the year 2010. Further a sanctioned strength of 60 students added in the year 2011 as second shift. The PG course in Power System Engineering Started with 18 sanctioned strength in the year 2007. The Department has 40 faculty members who are graduated from the top-notch universities/Colleges like NIT & IIT etc. The faculty members are consistently doing well in teaching and research. Faculty members and students frequently get awards, prizes for outstanding research contributions in their respective fields.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

The Department of Mechanical Engineering is one of the major departments established right from the inception of the college in 1997. The department has well experienced faculty and supporting staff members of proven ability and administrative skills. It has well equipped state-of-the-art laboratories that cater to the needs of the faculty and students.

CIVIL ENGINEERING

Established in the year 2009, the Department of Civil Engineering has made great strides during the short period of its existence. Under the able stewardship of Prof. Bijaya Kumar Rath, M.E. (IISc), Ph.D (IIT Kanpur), former Professor NIT, Rourkela, the Department has state-of-the-art laboratories and a mix of experienced and young faculty members. While Prof. R.K. Das, from ISRO, Prof. J.K. Pani, former Professor NIT Rourkela, Dr. G.Sridevi and Prof. S.S. Mohanty provide the experience, other young talented faculty members provide the drive to excel. Our faculty members are assigned with various theory subjects prepare course plans using the standard format provided by the institute with emphasis on 'learning' of the students. The instructional or lecture delivery of the faculty will be through a set of Educational Technology / Tools opted by the faculty.
